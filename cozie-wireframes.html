üé® Design Recommendations & UX Notes

  • Consistent Purple Theme: Maintain the purple gradient (#a855f7 to #8b5cf6) throughout the app as the primary brand color for buttons, accents, and highlights
  • Dark Mode First: The dark background (#1a1d29 to #2d1b3d gradient) creates an immersive music experience and reduces eye strain
  • Progressive Onboarding: Use progress dots to show users where they are in the sign-up/onboarding journey (4-6 steps max)
  • Social Integration: Connect music streaming services early to enable core sharing functionality
  • Music-First Feed: Album art should be prominent and visual, similar to Instagram but focused on music content
  • Quick Actions: Include heart/like, comment, share, and add-to-playlist actions on every music post
  • Discovery Features: Trending charts, genre-based exploration, and personalized recommendations based on preferences
  • Bottom Navigation: Use 5 primary tabs: Home (feed), Discover (search/explore), Share (post music), Messages, Profile
  • Engagement Mechanics: Show follower counts, post engagement metrics, and listening statistics to encourage activity
  • Optional Features: Allow users to skip non-essential steps (profile photo, bio, finding friends) to reduce friction
  • Real-time Updates: Show "2 hours ago", "now playing" status to create sense of live music sharing
  • Playlist Collaboration: Enable friends to build playlists together (collaborative feature for future)

üîÑ User Flow Summary

  • Step 1: User creates account with email/password
  • Step 2: Email verification via 6-digit code
  • Step 3: Select music genre preferences (minimum 3)
  • Step 4: Set up profile (photo, display name, bio)
  • Step 5: Connect streaming service (Spotify/Apple Music/SoundCloud)
  • Step 6: Find and follow friends
  • Step 7: Arrive at home feed with tutorial/welcome message
  • Step 8: User can browse, discover, share music, and engage with friends
